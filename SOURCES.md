# Sources & validation method

Every claim in this registry is traced to a UAE primary source — the underlying law, rulebook, or regulator-issued policy. Not legal newsletters. Not vendor blogs. Not AI summaries.

## Primary-source domains

| Domain | Used for |
|---|---|
| [uaelegislation.gov.ae](https://uaelegislation.gov.ae) | Federal laws and decree-laws (PDPL, Cybercrime Law, CBUAE DL 6/2025, Child Digital Safety) |
| [centralbank.ae](https://centralbank.ae) | CBUAE Rulebook and supervisory guidance |
| [dha.gov.ae](https://dha.gov.ae) | Dubai Health Authority — NABIDH policy |
| [doh.gov.ae](https://doh.gov.ae) | Abu Dhabi Department of Health — ADHICS |
| [mohap.gov.ae](https://mohap.gov.ae) | Ministry of Health & Prevention — Federal Health Data Law (FL 2/2019) |
| [tdra.gov.ae](https://tdra.gov.ae) | Telecommunications & Digital Government Regulatory Authority |
| [adgm.com](https://adgm.com) | ADGM Data Protection Regulations and Office of Data Protection |
| [difc.com](https://difc.com) | DIFC laws — DPL and amendments |
| [dfsa.ae](https://dfsa.ae) | DFSA Rulebook — GEN Module 5.5 Cyber Risk |
| [vara.ae](https://vara.ae) | Virtual Assets Regulatory Authority — Technology & Information Rulebook |
| [u.ae](https://u.ae) | Federal portal — cross-references and official announcements |

## Method

1. **Trace to statute.** Each row begins with the governing instrument (law, rulebook, regulation).
2. **Cross-check the regulator's published page.** Regulator pages often reflect subsequent amendments not yet propagated to legislation portals.
3. **Resolve conflicts.** Where sources disagree, we cite the most recent governing document and note the supersession date.
4. **Flag unknowns.** Where penalty figures are not yet specified by statute (e.g. PDPL pending Executive Regulations), we mark them as such rather than citing speculative ranges.
